About

  • I am Dr. Karanja Evanson, a lecturer at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT), specializing in the dynamic field of Computer Engineering. With a passion for both teaching and research, I am dedicated to equipping students with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology. Through engaging lectures, hands-on projects, and mentorship, I strive to inspire the next generation of computer engineers to push the boundaries of innovation and make meaningful contributions to society.
  • As a lecturer at JKUAT, I am not only focused on academic excellence but also on nurturing a culture of creativity, collaboration, and lifelong learning. I believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration and encourage my students to explore diverse perspectives and approaches to problem-solving.
  • Outside of the classroom, I am actively involved in research projects that address real-world challenges and push the boundaries of computer engineering. By bridging the gap between academia and industry, I aim to leverage technology for the social and economic development of our communities.
