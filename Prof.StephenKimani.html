About

  • As an ICT researcher, I am dedicated to exploring new frontiers in technology, seeking innovative ways to enhance user experiences and streamline information processes. My consultancy work involves providing expert advice and solutions to organizations seeking to optimize their ICT infrastructure and strategies. In my academic capacity, I am committed to impacting knowledge and skills to the next generation of ICT professionals. I believe in nurturing talent and fostering a culture of innovation and excellence among students.
  • As the ICT Director at JKUAT, I play a pivotal role in shaping the technological landscape of the institution. I oversee the implementation of ICT initiatives, ensuring that they align with the strategic goals of the university and contribute to its overall success.
  • Through my research, consultancy, academic, and leadership roles, I strive to make meaningful contributions to the field of ICT while empowering individuals and organizations to harness the power of technology for positive change.
