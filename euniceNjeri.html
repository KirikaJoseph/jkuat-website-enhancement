Dr. Eunice Njeri



With a background in both academia and industry, I bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to my role as an educator. My research and teaching interests lie at the intersection of technology and human behavior, where I explore how people interact with computers, robots, and other intelligent systems.

Contact

About

  • I am Dr. Eunice Njeri, a passionate lecturer at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT), specializing in the fascinating fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), and Social Robotics
  • In the classroom, I am dedicated to fostering a deep understanding of HCI, HRI, and social robotics among my students. Through hands-on projects, interactive discussions, and real-world case studies, I empower them to design, evaluate, and improve user experiences in a variety of contexts.
  • As a lecturer at JKUAT, I am committed to advancing the field of human-computer interaction and robotics through innovative research and collaboration. My work explores the social, ethical, and cultural implications of technology, with the aim of creating more inclusive and human-centered solutions
