About

    With a background in both academia and industry, I bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to my role as an educator. My research and teaching interests lie at the intersection of technology and human behavior, where I explore how people interact with computers, robots, and other intelligent systems.
    I am a passionate lecturer at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT), specializing in the fascinating fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), and Social Robotics
