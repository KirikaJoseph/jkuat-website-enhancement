About

    I am deeply committed to advancing knowledge in these areas and preparing the next generation of ICT professionals to navigate the complexities of the digital landscape. Through my teaching and research, I strive to provide students with the skills and insights they need to excel in a rapidly evolving field. My expertise in ICT policy allows me to analyze and shape the regulatory frameworks that govern technology use and adoption. I am passionate about promoting policies that foster innovation, protect privacy, and ensure equitable access to digital resources. In the realm of cloud computing, I explore the transformative potential of cloud technologies and their implications for businesses, governments, and society at large. My research delves into issues such as data security, scalability, and the economic impact of cloud adoption.
