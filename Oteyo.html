I am Mr. Isaac Oteyo, a dedicated lecturer at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT), where I am passionate about educating and inspiring the next generation of web developers.

About

  • As a lecturer, I am committed to providing students with hands-on experience in web development, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in today's digital economy. Through engaging lectures, practical exercises, and real-world projects, I empower my students to become proficient in PHP and other essential web technologies.
  • Beyond teaching, I am actively involved in research and development projects that push the boundaries of web technology . Whether it's exploring new frameworks, optimizing performance, or addressing security challenges, I am dedicated to advancing the field of web development through innovation and collaboration.
  • In addition to my technical skills, I am also passionate about fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment. I believe in the power of education to transform lives and empower individuals to reach their full potential. By nurturing creativity, critical thinking, and teamwork, I strive to inspire my students to become lifelong learners and passionate advocates for positive change.
