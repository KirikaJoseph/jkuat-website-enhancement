About

  • Dr. Lawrence Nderu (PhD) currently serves as the Chairman, Lecturer, and Researcher in AI/ML, specializing in Computer Science, Software Engineering, and Digital Ecosystem Development. With a keen focus on Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Software Development, Dr. Nderu's research interests lie in leveraging technology for building the resilience of communities. He is also the Founder of JKIAN Hub, a platform dedicated to fostering digital innovation and collaboration in Africa.
  • In his role, Dr. Nderu provides advisory and resource support for students, faculty, and external constituents interested in AI/ML, software engineering, and digital ecosystem development. He actively supports student organizational development and manages a collection of resources and event spaces at his institution. Collaborating with the JKIAN Hub team, Dr. Nderu coordinates events and supports the management and development of student organizations related to technology and digital innovation.
  • Dr. Nderu's dedication extends beyond academia, as he is deeply involved in community-building efforts and advocating for the responsible use of technology for societal advancement. Outside of his professional endeavors, Dr. Nderu enjoys exploring the local culinary scene and engaging in sports activities. Whether it's immersing himself in the vibrant tech ecosystem or savoring the flavors of African cuisine, Dr. Nderu embodies a holistic approach to his work and personal interests.
